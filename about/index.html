What is the Stale Scale?

The stale scale also known as the stalometer and known by fewer as the Moldy Meter, is the score assigned to a movie based on how awful it is. The more awful the movie, the higher the score. The scale ranges 0-10 from freshly baked, to burnt, to moldy, and to dumpster fire.
